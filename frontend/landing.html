WEB3 / BLOCKCHAIN

Decentralize Trust: Build the Future of Web3

PS 1: CRYPTOCURRENCY TRANSACTION TRACING

Problem: Crypto mixers and tumblers hide transaction trails, making it difficult to identify real fund receivers.

Solution: Build a blockchain analysis tool that traces transactions through mixing services and identifies the actual beneficiary using heuristic analysis and pattern recognition.

PS 2: DECENTRALIZED IDENTITY & CREDENTIALS

Problem: Students lack portable, verifiable credentials, and employers cannot instantly verify claimed skills.

Solution: Create a blockchain-based NFT credential system where institutions issue digital badges that employers can verify with one click.

PS 3: PEER-TO-PEER STUDENT LENDING PROTOCOL

Problem: Student debt is high, banks charge 6-8% interest, and no direct lending between alumni and students exists.

Solution: Build a DeFi lending protocol where alumni fund tuition in exchange for a share of future earnings, with smart contracts handling repayment logic.

PS 4: BLOCKCHAIN SUPPLY CHAIN TRANSPARENCY

Problem: Counterfeit goods hurt the economy, while consumers cannot verify authenticity and manufacturers cannot prove ethical sourcing.

Solution: Build an immutable blockchain tracker where every product journey is transparent and smart contracts reward ethical practices.

PS 5: DECENTRALIZED RENEWABLE ENERGY TRADING

Problem: Solar panel owners generate excess energy with nowhere to sell it peer-to-peer, while energy grids remain centralized and inefficient.

Solution: Create a blockchain marketplace where homeowners sell excess solar energy directly to neighbors with real-time smart contracts balancing supply and demand.

AI / ML

Master the Intelligence: Code the Future

PS 1: AI-DRIVEN CROP DISEASE PREDICTION

Problem: Crop diseases devastate yields leading to significant financial losses. Early detection and intervention are crucial but farmers lack access to diagnostic tools.

Solution: Build an AI system that analyzes crop images and environmental data to predict disease outbreaks and provide farmers with actionable treatment recommendations in real-time.

PS 2: PRICE PREDICTION FOR AGRICULTURAL COMMODITIES

Problem: Farmers face volatile prices for pulses, vegetables, and onions. Market unpredictability leads to income instability and poor planning for next season.

Solution: Develop ML models that predict commodity prices using historical trends, seasonality, weather patterns, and market data to help farmers make informed selling decisions.

PS 3: FACIAL RECOGNITION FOR FAKE SOCIAL MEDIA ACCOUNTS

Problem: Fake social media accounts spread misinformation, facilitate harassment, and enable fraud. Manual detection is slow and inefficient.

Solution: Build AI system that identifies fake profiles on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter using facial recognition, behavior patterns, and account metadata to flag accounts for suspension.

PS 4: AI-POWERED EARLY WARNING SYSTEM FOR STUDENT DROPOUT

Problem: Students at risk of dropping out aren't identified early enough. Dropout rates disproportionately affect marginalized communities.

Solution: Create AI model that analyzes attendance, grades, behavior data to predict at-risk students and trigger timely interventions from teachers and counselors.

PS 5: PERSONALIZED AI CAREER GUIDANCE SYSTEM

Problem: Students struggle to find right career paths aligned with their interests, skills, and market demands. Traditional counseling lacks personalization.

Solution: Build AI system that assesses student aptitude, aspirations, abilities, and recommends personalized career pathways with skill gap analysis and targeted learning opportunities.

CLOUD / DEVOPS

Scale Without Limits: Engineer the Cloud

PS 1: INTELLIGENT CONTAINER ORCHESTRATION & AUTO-SCALING

Problem: Applications face unpredictable traffic spikes that cause crashes, while manual scaling is slow and costly.

Solution: Build an ML-powered system that predicts traffic demand and automatically scales containerized applications across cloud infrastructure.

PS 2: CLOUD COST OPTIMIZATION ENGINE

Problem: Cloud bills grow because of unused resources, inefficient deployments, and poor allocation decisions.

Solution: Create an AI system that analyzes cloud usage, identifies waste, recommends rightsizing, and automates cost optimization.

PS 3: DEPLOYMENT PIPELINE AUTOMATION & FAILURE PREDICTION

Problem: Manual deployments create downtime, errors, and slow rollbacks that reduce team productivity.

Solution: Build an intelligent CI/CD platform that automates deployments, predicts failures before release, and supports instant rollback.

PS 4: MULTI-CLOUD WORKLOAD MANAGEMENT PLATFORM

Problem: Managing workloads across AWS, Azure, and GCP is fragmented, making visibility, cost tracking, and migration difficult.

Solution: Develop a unified platform for monitoring, managing, and orchestrating workloads across multiple cloud providers.

PS 5: INFRASTRUCTURE MONITORING & PREDICTIVE ALERTING

Problem: Traditional monitoring is reactive, so teams learn about issues only after downtime or customer complaints.

Solution: Build an observability platform with predictive anomaly detection to identify failures before they impact services.

CYBERSECURITY

Defend the Digital Frontier: Secure What Matters

PS 1: DDOS PROTECTION & DETECTION FOR CLOUD INFRASTRUCTURE

Problem: Cloud-hosted applications are disrupted by DDoS attacks, and current detection methods are slow and manual.

Solution: Build an automated system that detects DDoS attacks from traffic patterns, identifies sources, and triggers mitigation in real time.

PS 2: PII DETECTION IN UPLOADED DOCUMENTS

Problem: Users upload documents containing sensitive Aadhaar, PAN, and driving license data that can expose personal information.

Solution: Develop an AI-based system that detects PII in uploaded documents and alerts users before the information is exposed.

PS 3: VULNERABILITY SCANNING FOR OEM EQUIPMENT

Problem: Critical organizations rely on legacy IT/OT equipment while vulnerability updates are scattered across OEM websites and delayed.

Solution: Build a monitoring tool that continuously tracks OEM sites for critical and high-severity vulnerabilities and sends instant alerts.

PS 4: CYBER INCIDENT THREAT INTELLIGENCE FEED

Problem: Organizations lack real-time visibility into cyber threats affecting Indian digital infrastructure and depend on delayed intelligence sources.

Solution: Create a threat intelligence system that collects and analyzes incident data from forums, paste sites, social media, and darknet sources.

PS 5: DARK WEB OPERATOR DE-ANONYMIZATION

Problem: Dark web marketplaces operate anonymously, enabling illegal trade in drugs, weapons, and counterfeit goods without accountability.

Solution: Develop a technique or tool to identify hidden operators by tracing infrastructure, network clues, and related identity artifacts.
